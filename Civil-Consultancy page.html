About Civil Consultancy Cell


The Department of Civil Engineering has been in the forefront, since its inception, in offering its expertise to provide solutions to the field problems. Many public sector undertakings and private companies consult the Department for technical advice. Highly experienced and dedicated faculties are the icons of our Institute. The Department has also excellent interaction with various State and Central Government Departments and several Municipal bodies and Urban Development Authorities.
sr. Certification Certification Assignment
1 Schedule-I (Environmental Auditor) Gujarat Pollution Control Board approved Environment Auditor for Schedule-I category Industry Environmental Auditing of Industries to measure different parameters of Ambient air, Stack Monitoring, Potable Water, Waste Water, Hazardous Waste and Noise Parameter.
2 NABL - National Accreditation Board for Testing and Calibration Laboratories Environmental Laboratory is accredited in accordance with ISO/IEC 17025:2017 as Chemical Testing Laboratory by National Accreditation Board for Testing and Calibration Laboratories (NABL) for the defined scope of sampling and analysis of Ambient Air Quality, Stack Emission, Waste Water & Drinking Water.
3 Material Testing Laboratory R & B - Road & Building department(Government of Gujarat) Approved Laboratory for Testing of Construction Material like Cement, Sand, Soil, Aggregate, Brick, Steel, Bitumen, Concrete, Bitumen mix and Pavement Evaluation using Bankleman Beam deflection technique.
4 RUDA - Rajkot Urban Development Authority Testing of in-situ soil for Safe Bearing Capacity by boring, Testing of Construction Material and Workmanship Testing which includes Quality control and Quality assurance of construction work.
5 NABL - National Accreditation Board for testing and calibration laboratories Testing of building material such as Cement, Bricks, Tiles, Paver Block, Light Weight Block, Bitumen, Concrete, Bitumen mix and soil testing.
6 GIDC – Gujarat Industrial Development Corporation GIDC has approved Darshan Consultancy Cell for testing of materials and preparation of mix designs for work which are being done by various contractors.
7 AAI – Airport Authorities of India Airport Authority of India has approved Darshan Institute as material testing laboratory for testing of various materials such as Concrete and its mix design, Bitumen and its mix design, Blocks and Bricks, Soil and Aggregates and Reinforcement for construction of Runway and other facilities of Airports.
8 Environmental Auditor (Schedule-I) Certification Services Inc. VRC Quality Excellence Providing testing and consultancy services in field of civil engineering related to Building Material & design, Highway Materials and design, Bridge Material and design, Soil testing, Geotechnical Investigation and Foundation design, Water testing and analysis and testing of mechanical properties of industrial products.
