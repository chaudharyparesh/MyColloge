About Energy Management Cell


In this cost competitive globe for energy conservation in industry and commerce, the Electrical Engineering Department is running Energy Management Cell (www.emc.darshan.ac.in). We are an independent service provider that offers the most advanced and best in Energy Conservation Solutions with the thrust in the key areas of Energy Audit Services, Power Quality Audit Services, Electrical Safety Audit Services, Thermography, Electrical Earthing System Design and Measurement, Solar Power Plant EPC and Other Consultancy Services.

We have a successful and satisfied customer base of the products and solutions in the Commercial and Industrial segment. We are supported by the state-of-the-art infrastructure that further helps us in delivering quality services to clients. With world-class products and excellent services, DIET has been constantly innovating its services to suit the changing scenario and in keeping with this focus, has launched a specialized Energy Conservation Solutions to assist businesses with their various Power Quality risks. We engage ourselves in developing, installing and financing comprehensive performance-based projects.




In correlation with above said we are providing subsequent energy related services which identifies the industrial expectation and promotes institutional preparation for meeting industrial needs.
Energy Related Services Activities Performed
Investigative Expertise Energy Audit, Energy Conservation, Energy Management
Equipment Failure Analysis Electric Motors, Electric Generators, Refrigeration, Compressor, Electrical Furnaces
Electrical Measurements & Design Voltage, Current, Power Factor, kW, kVAR, kVA, Maximum Demand, Transients, THD
Electrical Safety Audit Electrical and Static Electricity Hazards In Plant
Thermal Imaging Transmission Line, Generator, Electric Motor, Transformers, Switchgear, Solar Panels, UPS, Mechanical Coupling, Thermal Utilities
PV Solar Power Plant EPC Detailed planning, design, execution and power quality monitoring of the solar power plant
Electrical Earthing System Design Detailed planning, design, execution of earthing systems. 3-Pole, 4-Pole, 2 Clamps Earth Resistance measurement. Soil Resistivity measurement
Pump & Motor Testing Efficiency measurement of pumping installation
Electrical Design & Drafting Building Electrical Wiring and Electrical Lighting Design
Electrical Distribution Design Electrical Distribution Design
